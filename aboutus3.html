✨ About the Podcast

Hi, I’m Angel — and this is FarrahLove Podcast. This space is all about real talk: love, marriage, relationships, family, and life advice.

My background started in nursing, where I learned how deeply health connects with our personal lives. Along the way, I picked up an interest in psychology, and most importantly, I became a listener. A connector. Someone people naturally open up to.

This podcast isn’t about lectures or perfect answers — it’s about stories, conversations, and the lessons we pick up along the way. Whether you’re single, navigating love, or just figuring out life, you’ll find honest, unfiltered talks here.

So grab your headphones, settle in, and let’s explore life together — one episode at a time. 🎙️

FarrahLove is about empowering hearts, families, and growth through love, stories, and shared journeys.
